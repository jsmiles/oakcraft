About Us

We are a family run bespoke furniture maker based in Tyneham, Dorset. We were founded in 1921 and across five generations have taken pride in our passion for hand crafted furniture. In our showroom we do stock many popular items ready for sale. However, we specialise in bespoke furniture. Speak to use today about a design or pattern you might be interested in commissioning.
